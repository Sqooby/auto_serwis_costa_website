# Business Requirements

## 1. Business Purpose of the Website

The website serves as a comprehensive digital platform with the following objectives:

### Primary Purposes

1. **Professional Online Presence**
   - Establish credibility and professionalism for "COSTA" Auto Service workshop
   - Create a modern, trustworthy digital footprint
   - Differentiate from competitors through quality presentation

2. **Information Hub**
   - Provide instant access to contact information
   - Display complete service offerings
   - Show accurate location and opening hours
   - Enable quick customer decision-making

3. **Trust Building Platform**
   - Leverage strong Google rating (4.8/5 from 55 reviews)
   - Showcase real customer testimonials
   - Demonstrate expertise and reliability
   - Build confidence in potential customers

4. **Lead Generation Tool**
   - Attract new clients searching for mechanics in Rzeszów
   - Capture inquiries through contact forms
   - Facilitate phone calls with click-to-call functionality
   - Convert website visitors into paying customers

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Audience Segments

#### 2.1. Local Car Owners
- **Location:** Rzeszów and surrounding areas
- **Vehicle Type:** All car makes and models
- **Need:** Regular maintenance, repairs, and service

#### 2.2. A/C Service Seekers
- **Profile:** Drivers experiencing air conditioning issues
- **Season:** Peak demand in spring/summer
- **Requirements:** Refrigerant refills, leak detection, system cleaning

#### 2.3. Diagnostic & Repair Customers
- **Pain Point:** Car problems requiring expert diagnosis
- **Need:** Computer diagnostics, mechanical repairs
- **Services:** Brakes, suspension, starters, alternators

#### 2.4. Trust-Seeking Customers
- **Background:** Often had bad experiences with other mechanics
- **Values:** Honesty, transparency, fair pricing
- **Decision Factors:** Reviews, reputation, clear communication

### Audience Characteristics

**Demographics:**
- Age: 25-65 years
- Gender: All
- Income: Middle class
- Location: Urban and suburban Rzeszów

**Psychographics:**
- Value reliability and honesty
- Prefer clear explanations over technical jargon
- Appreciate fair, non-inflated pricing
- Seek long-term mechanic relationships
- Trust peer reviews and recommendations

**Behavior Patterns:**
- Search online for "mechanic near me"
- Read Google reviews before choosing service
- Call directly when urgent repair needed
- Compare prices and services
- Value convenience and accessibility

## 7. Customer Journey Map

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Customer Need:** Car problem or maintenance due
**Touchpoint:** Google search, word of mouth
**Goal:** Appear in local search results

### Stage 2: Consideration
**Customer Action:** Reviews website, reads reviews
**Touchpoint:** Website, Google Business Profile
**Goal:** Build trust through reviews and clear information

### Stage 3: Decision
**Customer Action:** Contacts workshop
**Touchpoint:** Phone call, contact form
**Goal:** Make contact easy and immediate

### Stage 4: Service
**Customer Action:** Visits workshop for repair
**Touchpoint:** In-person service
**Goal:** Exceed expectations with quality work

### Stage 5: Loyalty
**Customer Action:** Returns for future service
**Touchpoint:** Follow-up, reminders
**Goal:** Build long-term relationship
