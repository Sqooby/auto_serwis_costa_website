### 4.3 URL Structure

**Best Practices:**
```
✓ https://example.com/uslugi/klimatyzacja
✓ https://example.com/opinie
✓ https://example.com/kontakt

✗ https://example.com/page?id=123
✗ https://example.com/uslugi-klimatyzacji-samochodowej-rzeszow-costa
```

**URL Guidelines:**
- Use Polish characters (ą, ę, ć, etc.) or convert to ASCII
- Keep URLs short and descriptive
- Include primary keyword when natural
- Use hyphens, not underscores
- All lowercase
